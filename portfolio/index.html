About

Your Trusted Scoping Partner

I'm Stephanie Allen, a dedicated legal scopist and transcriptionist based in Tampa, FL, with over 8 years of experience supporting court reporters and law firms across the country. Through my agency, Allen Legal Scoping Services, I deliver transcripts that reflect your style, your standards, and your deadlines — every time.

I work with Case CATalyst, FTR, JAVS, and Escribe, and I'm proficient in synchronized audio workflows that ensure exceptional precision. I follow Morson's English Guide for Court Reporters and adapt quickly to each reporter's individual preference sheet.

Whether you need standard scoping or a same-day rush, I pride myself on clear communication, confidentiality, and results you can count on.

What I Do

Services

Legal Scoping

Full audio scoping for court reporters, including research of names, correction of spelling, grammar, and formatting errors — aligned precisely to your preference sheet.

Legal Transcription

Accurate transcription of depositions, hearings, arbitrations, and trials. Compatible with Zoom audio, FTR, JAVS, and external audio files from videographers.

Proofreading & Editing

Meticulous review of completed transcripts for consistency, accuracy, and court-ready formatting. Verbatim but tidied up — exactly how clients expect it.

Policies

Rates are based on up to 25 lines per page.

Clear audio required prior to submission. Audio that is unclear is subject to an additional per-page rate.

Payment required immediately for first-time and one-off assignments.

Business days: Monday–Friday, 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Mountain Time.

Billing

Invoices sent bi-weekly on the 1st and 15th of each month, with payment due within 15 days.

An itemized invoice arrives on the 1st of each month for the prior month's work.

Preferred payment methods: PayPal and Zelle.

A reminder will be sent before payment is due on any outstanding invoices.
